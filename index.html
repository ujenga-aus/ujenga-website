Claim Leverage & Preparation Intelligence

Our AI analyses your project record against the contract, programme and specification to identify and structure the strongest grounds for entitlement - delays, disruptions, scope additions and principal-caused events. It surfaces supporting evidence across the document suite, maps the causation chain and drafts preliminary claim narratives grounded in your own project record. Experienced specialist review then shapes the strategy. The volume AI can process in hours would take weeks manually - which means more of your claim record gets examined, not sampled.

Monte Carlo Risk Modelling

Our in-house Monte Carlo simulation engine ingests your contract, scope of work and project specifications to build a probabilistic risk model calibrated to your specific project - not a generic template. The risk categories, weightings and drivers are informed by 30+ years of seeing which risks actually materialise on infrastructure programmes and what they cost. The model runs thousands of simulations to quantify cost and programme outcomes, confidence intervals and the commercial sensitivity of each risk. Specialist judgment determines what goes in. The AI handles the computation at a scale and speed no spreadsheet can match.

AI eDiscovery - Outlook & Email Intelligence

Some of the most valuable evidence on a construction project never makes it into a formal register. It lives in email inboxes - in the instructions given, the warnings ignored, the approvals granted and the complaints made, often years before a dispute crystallises. Our AI eDiscovery module ingests Outlook PST files and email archives, then searches, tags and structures the correspondence record against your specific claim issues, events and timeframes.

The system surfaces emails that corroborate or undermine positions, identifies key threads, extracts admissions and flags unanswered notices - producing a structured chronology and evidence index that a specialist can work from directly. Email evidence that would take a team weeks to manually review is processed, organised and prioritised in a fraction of the time.

PST File Ingestion Email Archive Search Evidence Chronology Claim Corroboration Notice Identification

Scope Intelligence Module

Contracts, specifications and scope documents carry dozens - sometimes hundreds - of obligations, hold points, witness points, submission requirements and key references that a project team must actively manage. In practice, these are rarely captured systematically at project start-up, and the consequences of missing them can be severe: loss of entitlement, non-compliance, or an undermined claim position.

Our Scope Intelligence module reads the full contract and specification suite and produces a structured obligations register: every obligation identified, categorised, cross-referenced to its source clause and mapped to the relevant project phase. Hold points and witness points are extracted and scheduled. The output is a practical management plan your project team can actually use from day one - not a document sitting on a shelf.
